3. Methodology & Standards Followed

This engagement combined OSINT-driven brand monitoring with a lightweight passive technical assessment, aligned to:

  • OWASP Top 10 (2021) & OWASP ASVS for application risk classification
  • OWASP MASVS for mobile app threat surface
  • PTES & NIST SP 800-115 for security testing methodology
  • MITRE ATT&CK for adversary technique mapping
